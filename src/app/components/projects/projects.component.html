Save the Coral Reef

Final Year Project At Monash University (Team Project)
A website developed using Node.js, Express.js and Angular.js. BootStrap was adopted for the UI design.

Researched habitats and ecosystem of coral reefs in the Australian Ocean, and several factors that affect the coral reefs by analysing datasets. Regular users were offered helpful information based on the analysis and live monitoring system.

Dog and Cookies

Side Project At Monash University (Personal Project)
Simple game developed using Unreal Engine 5 with C++.

Players are spawned with a pet and are required to collect cookies on the ground before arriving at the destination. Enemies, weapons, cookies and obstacles are spawned from BluePrints.
